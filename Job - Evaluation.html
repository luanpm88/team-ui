    Interview 1
    😃 Small talkThey may be nervous or just ran from the bus stop to make the appointment. Start with friendly small talk and chatter to break the ice and allow them to catch their nerves and find some calm - Interviews can be daunting.🤩 Excite them with WHY they should want to work here! Assume they've applied for 10 other jobs... Introduce yourself and your journey with us, tell them about your team and what hobbies you all have and why you love working here. This creates excitement and fear of missing out. It creates a vision of the people they'd be working with and subtly positions your role and team as their preferred job to get accepted to. 😎 Let them interview you! At the end, tell them they can now interview you to find out more about the details they want to know about❓ "Is there anything about what we've discussed today that's put you off, or made you a bit unsure about the role?💡Let's talk about it now so we can make sure I've explained everything in the right way for you" These are powerful questions to ensure we can overcome any misinterpretations in the conversations
